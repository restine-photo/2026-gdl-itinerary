Guadalajara & Tequila Valley

Proposed Itinerary

Draft status: hotel, tours, and restaurant reservations are all placeholders below — nothing is confirmed. Treat this as a shape for the week, not a locked schedule.
1
Friday, Nov 20
Arrival Early landing

Land around 6am — GDL and Chicago share a time zone, so it's an early morning rather than jet lag. Uber to the hotel in Colonia Americana / Chapultepec by ~7:30-8am.

Ask about early check-in, or drop bags at the front desk and use the morning for a slow coffee walk around the neighborhood.

Rest once the room's ready. Easy, early dinner at La Posta.

2
Saturday, Nov 21
Centro Histórico

Morning walking tour with Camina GDL — Cathedral, Teatro Degollado, Hospicio Cabañas.

Dinner at Elefante de Piedra, drinks after at Tiny Bar Speakeasy.

3
Sunday, Nov 22
Tlaquepaque

Day trip to the artisan village — galleries, ceramics, glassblowing, wandering Calle Independencia.

Dinner at Brasa y Sal back in Chapultepec.

4
Monday, Nov 23
Move to Tequila town Change of base

Transfer out to the town of Tequila (~1 hr). Check into Casa Salles Hotel or Hotel Gloriagave for two nights.

Casa Salles sits right next to El Tequileño distillery — an easy, low-effort visit the same afternoon if staying there.

Dinner at La Taberna del Cofrade, the underground tavern-style restaurant.

5
Tuesday, Nov 24
Distillery day Headliners

Arette (El Llano Distillery) in the morning — Orendain family, over 125 years independent, additive-free small-batch.

La Fortaleza midday — Sauza family descendants, the most traditional production method in the valley, tasting inside their cave.

If there's time and energy left, Cascahuín in El Arenal is a short hop and already a favorite from the saved list.

Vive Tequila Tour or Explora México Tours can likely arrange the logistics for this route.

6
Wednesday, Nov 25
Back to Guadalajara

Transfer back to GDL, check into the city hotel for the remaining nights.

Easy afternoon, relaxed dinner — good night to just wing it based on what's nearby.

7
Thursday, Nov 26
Flex day

Open day — more Chapultepec exploring, shopping, or a repeat visit to a favorite spot from earlier in the week.

8
Friday, Nov 27
Last full day Send-off dinner

Wrap-up day — anything missed, souvenirs, packing.

Good candidate for a last-hurrah dinner, since there's nowhere to be early the next morning but the airport.

9
Saturday, Nov 28
Departure

Fly back to Chicago.
